Guidelines

If you still want to submit your track despite these harsh conditions, the guidelines are below~

I will review every track that is submitted and contact you if I like the track. This part is obviously very subjective. You could send a beautifully mastered track but I simply don't like because the BPM is too fast. Likewise, the song could be very repetitive with only one notable sample, and I might just feature that track first in the stream.

I will only be accepting ONE track from each individual/group. This is to make it fair for other people submitting their tracks

1. All samples in your track must be cleared beforehand. Obviously this doesnt apply if your track:

Consists mostly of YOUR instrumentals

Consists mostly of YOUR vocals

2. If your track is part of a collective/record label, please provide contact information.

3. Send a MP3 or WAV file. If that is not possible, explain why and send another format. Please click the links if you need more information.

If you think your stream meets the requirements, contact me here:
